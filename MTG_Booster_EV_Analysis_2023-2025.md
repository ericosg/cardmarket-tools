## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This comprehensive analysis reveals remarkable expected value opportunities across recent MTG sets from 2023-2025. Of the 20 sets analyzed, **all 20 have positive EV**, with ratios ranging from 1.52x to an extraordinary 19.61x.

**Key Findings:**
- **2023 dominates value:** Contains the highest EV sets overall, with Lord of the Rings (19.61x) and Lost Caverns of Ixalan (10.91x) leading all years
- **2025 offers best budget opportunities:** Aetherdrift and Dragonstorm provide 9+ EV ratios at competitive prices

- **Average EV by year:** 2023 (8.87x), 2025 (6.69x), 2024 (4.53x)

The standout observation is that **every analyzed set has positive expected value**, suggesting a strong secondary market for singles across the board. Lord of the Rings: Tales of Middle-earth represents the single best value proposition with nearly 20x EV ratio.

## METHODOLOGY

**EV Calculation:**
- Expected Value based on Scryfall card prices (EUR)
- Uses exact booster collation rules (rarity distributions)
- Bulk threshold: €1.00 (cards below this excluded from EV)
- Foil premiums included where applicable
- Data sourced from 98,349 cards across 739 sets
- 374 expansion mappings with 8 sets having detailed collation rules

**Pricing:**
- Cardmarket average prices (export data)
- Updated November 6, 2025 (13 hours old)
- Represents typical market cost across European sellers
- May vary by seller, condition, and region

**Limitations:**
- EV assumes perfect singles sales at market price
- Doesn't account for selling fees, shipping, time investment
- Variance can be significant (lucky/unlucky boxes)
- EV changes as card prices fluctuate over time
- Some chase cards create extreme variance (e.g., The One Ring)
- Results represent mathematical expectation, not guaranteed returns
- 3 sets unavailable: Assassin's Creed, Spider-Man, Avatar

**Box Configuration Assumptions:**
- Most 2023 sets: 36 packs per Draft Booster Box
- Most 2024-2025 sets: 36 packs per Play Booster Box
- Commander Masters: 24 packs per box
- Modern Horizons 3: 24 packs per box
- Per-booster costs calculated by dividing box price by pack count

## GLOSSARY

- **Box EV:** Total expected value of all cards in the box based on singles prices
- **EV Ratio:** Box EV divided by box cost (>1.0 = positive EV, profitable on average)
- **Profit per Booster:** Expected profit divided by pack count (profit/variance indicator)
- **Status:**
  - ✓ Pos: Positive EV (ratio ≥ 1.2) - Profitable to crack for singles
  - ~: Break even (ratio 1.0-1.2) - Minimal profit margin
  - ✗ Neg: Negative EV (ratio < 1.0) - Lose money on average
- **Avg Price:** Cardmarket average selling price (typical market value)
- **Low Price:** Lowest available price on Cardmarket (budget option)
- **Trend Price:** Cardmarket trend price (recent sales direction)
